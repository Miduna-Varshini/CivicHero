## 📋 Problem Statement

Communities frequently face issues such as potholes, water leakages, damaged streetlights, and waste management concerns. Reporting these problems is fragmented, difficult to track, and lacks transparency — resulting in civic neglect and community frustration.

The challenge is to build a platform where citizens can **identify, report, validate, track, and resolve** community issues through collaboration, data, and intelligent automation while encouraging transparency, accountability, and community participation.

---

## 💡 Solution — CivicHero

CivicHero is a **mobile-first, fully responsive** community web application that bridges the gap between neighborhood residents and local municipal authorities.

Every reported issue is visible to the entire community, validated through upvote collaboration, and tracked through a clear three-stage resolution pipeline:

**`Active` → `Working` → `Fixed`**

The platform prioritizes transparency, accountability, and community participation at every layer of its design.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### Real-Time Impact Dashboard
Live metrics panel at the top of the interface displaying three dynamic counters — **Active**, **Working**, and **Fixed**. These update instantly whenever any issue changes state, giving every community member a bird's-eye view of their neighborhood's infrastructure health.

### Community Upvote Validation
Every reported issue card includes an upvote counter that allows community members to verify the legitimacy of a report. This crowd-sourced validation filters out spam and ensures high-priority structural problems rise to the top.

### Color-Coded Priority Badges
Each issue is tagged with a priority badge — **HIGH**, **MEDIUM**, or **LOW** — in distinct colors. This allows citizens and administrators to immediately identify critical hazards requiring urgent attention.

### Inline Edit & Delete
Issue creators have full control through a clean **three-dots contextual menu** on every card — allowing them to Edit or Delete their report. Changes reflect instantly across the dashboard counters in real time.

### Targeted Notification Hub
A notification bell in the header acts as the community's broadcast channel. Alerts are sent **only when an issue is officially marked as Fixed** — celebrating real community wins and keeping residents informed about genuine progress.

### Premium Floating Issue Cards
Each problem is displayed in a high-contrast floating card with deep shadow styling and modern rounded corners — containing the issue title, description, category, location, priority badge, upvote count, and action menu.
